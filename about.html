About Us

ABOUT THE COMPANY

BOITHA HOLDINGS (PTY) LTD is the visionary creation of Lerato Mtolo, a dynamic young Black woman entrepreneur. While working as a Chemist at Anglo Coal Laboratory, Lerato identified a critical gap in managing emotions and interpersonal dynamics within the corporate environment. Driven by a desire to make a difference, she volunteered in the company’s Wellness Department, where she developed comprehensive programs on Team Building, HIV Awareness, Emotional Wellbeing, Safety Campaigns, and Conflict Management. Her initiatives led to significant positive changes among employees, and she was subsequently appointed as the Anglo Coal Wellness Coordinator. Leveraging her expertise as a qualified Laboratory Chemist, Lerato eventually resigned to establish BOITHA Motivational Speaking, which later evolved into BOITHA HOLDINGS.

Lerato is now an internationally certified Leadership Coach, Speaker, and Facilitator, trained under ICAN and John Maxwell. She has translated her entrepreneurial journey into a mission-driven career, guided by her philosophy: "A natural gift is not effective or impactful until it is qualified."

Recognizing another industry gap, BOITHA HOLDINGS expanded into the trucking and logistics sector. Lerato observed inefficiencies while managing a subcontracted trucking operation in Pretoria and Centurion, particularly regarding punctuality, delivery reliability, and communication between contractors and subcontractors. Through BOITHA HOLDINGS t/a BOITHA Trucking, Lerato is addressing these challenges, providing professional, high-quality service delivery, and setting a new standard in the logistics industry.
