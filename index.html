SUMMARY

Lerato began her professional career in 2007 as a Junior Analyst at the Impala Platinum Laboratory. In 2009, she advanced to the role of Senior Chemical Analyst at Anglo Thermal Coal. Driven by a strong work ethic and a consistently positive outlook, she later volunteered at Australian Laboratory Services as a Chemical Technician. Her commitment and technical excellence led to her appointment as a Senior Chemical Technician, reporting directly to the Laboratory Manager.

Today, Lerato is a respected motivational speaker and leadership coach, committed to transforming mindsets and empowering individuals and organizations to achieve sustainable growth and reach their full potential.
